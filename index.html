About Us

Our logo represents DEN-DAHAN, a small sari-sari store managed by Nanay Denia in Real St., Barangay Talibong, Cabucgayan, Biliran. The name combines “DEN” for Denia and “DAHAN” from *tindahan* (store in Tagalog). The logo features a house-like store icon, symbolizing its location in Nanay Denia's home. The "We Are Open" sign conveys a welcoming vibe, while the girl at the door represents Nanay Denia's friendly presence. The soft green color palette symbolizes growth, trust, and comfort, creating an inviting atmosphere for the community.

  • MISSION

    To provide essential products and basic needs to our community with a welcoming and friendly atmosphere. We also offer comfort, ensuring that every customer feels valued and supported.

  • VISION

    To become successful and a favorite local store that represent growth, trustworthiness, and hope.
